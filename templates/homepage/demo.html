The following are screen shots of a soon to be released early demonstration of Map of Life that provides initial and very simple, core functionality. In this first demo release, Map of Life will provide the means to search on a common or scientific name and get a list of resulting geographic distribution products based on full available taxonomy. We currently provide access to expert opinion range maps, point presence data made available by the Global Biodiversity Information Facility, and species presences in protected areas and ecoregions. Using simple map tools, you may explore these maps using familiar tools, and see how these different sources of data agree or disagree. You may also learn a bit more about what species are found at a point on the globe using a species list tool.
